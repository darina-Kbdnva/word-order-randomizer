Language Learning Tool:

Word Order Randomizer

Digital Flashcards to practice new vocabulary through the Language Learning Tool:

Type in the word you want to practice and its meaning. Press save to write it down.
Then, press shuffle and they will appear in a completely random order.
Now, try to remember the meaning of the words without peecking.
When you hover over a word, it'll show the meaning you have assigned to it!
Go forth in your language leaning journey! Rememeber that practice makes perfect, scpecially in things like these.
